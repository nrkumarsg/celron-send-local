AirCable Manual

1. How to Connect

Ensure both devices (phones, laptops, etc.) are connected to the same Wi-Fi network or Local Hotspot. Simply open the app and they will securely find each other. Transfers happen instantly over your router.

2. Renaming Your Device

In the top right corner, your device will be given a random network name (like Desktop 1234). You must click this name to rename it (e.g., "Chief Engineer Laptop"). This ensures your colleagues don't accidentally send confidential files to the wrong person on the network!

3. Global Sharing (Different Networks)

If you have internet access and want to send files to someone far away, click the Key Icon at the top right. Agree on a 4-digit code with the receiver (e.g., "5555"). When both of you enter it, your devices will pair securely across the internet.

4. Mid-Sea (Offline) Usage

AirCable works 100% offline. Just plug in the provided USB Executable, double-click it on your laptop, and tell your colleagues to connect their phones to that laptop's Local Wi-Fi IP address shown on the screen.

5. Fast Sharing (WhatsApp-Style)

Tired of searching for files in folders? You can now Copy any image or file and simply Paste (Ctrl+V) anywhere on this screen. AirCable will detect it instantly and ask you where to send it. It is the fastest way to share!

Pro Tip: Multiple Tabs Issue
If you see your device name appearing 3 or 4 times, it means you have AirCable open in **multiple browser tabs**. Each tab counts as a separate "Nearby Device". Close the extra tabs to see only one clean node!
